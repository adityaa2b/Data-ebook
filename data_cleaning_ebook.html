Missing Values

Empty cells or incomplete records that leave gaps in your dataset

Example: Customer records missing email addresses or phone numbers
2

Duplicates

Repeated entries that distort analysis and inflate metrics

Example: Same customer appearing multiple times with slight name variations
3

Inconsistent Formats

Dates, currencies, or units recorded differently across the dataset

Example: Dates as "12/25/2024", "25-Dec-2024", "Dec 25, 2024"
4

Outliers

Extreme values that may skew results or indicate data entry errors

Example: Age entered as 999 instead of 99, or salary as $9,999,999
5

Noise

Irrelevant or erroneous data points that don't add value to analysis

Example: Random characters, test entries, or placeholder values
